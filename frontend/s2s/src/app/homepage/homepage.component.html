In response to the loneliness epidemic identified by the United States Department of Health and Human Services in 2023, Shoulder to Shoulder was created to combat social isolation by fostering meaningful connections.

Getting started is simple

  1. Create an Account: Sign up and join our community.
  2. Onboard with Us: Dive into our onboarding process, where you'll complete preference surveys and set your event availability.
  3. Set Up Your Profile: Customize your profile with personal information and a profile picture.
  4. Create Events: Got an idea for an event? Set it up on our platform and invite others!
  5. Receive Recommendations: Based on your preferences and interactions, receive personalized event recommendations.
  6. Attend Events: Step out and enjoy! Participate in events, meet new people, and build lasting relationships.
